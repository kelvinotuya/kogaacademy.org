What We Do

Street Business School

Micro, small and medium scale businesses accounts for the largest portion of the Nigerian economy but unfortunately, most of them remain below the poverty line.

The Street Business School initiative by Koga Academy will bridge this gap by teaching basic entrepreneurial skills in simple terms from branding to money management, wealth creation, etc.

Courses will be thought to candidates our pilot program. The content generated will be re-produced and made available to an unrestricted number of people in our target demography across Nigeria and beyond.

Certificates will be awarded after the training program with the support of Our Technical partners: SMEDAN.
